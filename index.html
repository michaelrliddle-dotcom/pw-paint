About PW Paint

BUILT BY PAINTERS.
RUN FOR RESULTS.

PW Paint was founded in 2013 by a crew of working painters who spent years watching commercial jobs go wrong from rushed prep and cheap materials. We built the company on a single standard: do the prep right, use the right products, and stand behind the work in writing.

Today we serve general contractors, property management firms, HOAs, restaurant groups, and retail brands across Los Angeles, Orange County, and San Diego County.

  • W-2 employees on every project, zero subcontractors
  • Written 5-year warranty on all labor and materials
